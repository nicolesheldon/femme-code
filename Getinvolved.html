Get Involved NOW

Find out how you can get involved in and outside of St Andrews!

HeForShe is a solidarity campaign for the advancement of gender equality. It was initiated by the UN. Read a recent article about HeForShe in St Andrews in the Saint or visit their facebook page @HeForSheStAndrews to find out more.

Women in Work Women in Work is a St Andrews based society founded in 2014 with the aim of broadening womens' outlook on the job world.Read a recent article about in the Tab or visit their facebook page @womeninworksta to find out more.

Women for Women International is a nonprofit humanitarian organization that provides practical and moral support to women survivors of war. Visit their facebook page to find out more.

Her Choice aims to raise funds for thousands of women who are unable to access abortion facilities and to raise awareness of their plight. Visit their facebook page @herchoicestandrews to find out more.

Women in Computer Science is a St Andrews based society representing women studying or interested in computer science. Visit their facebook page @standrewswomenincs to find out more.

Feminist Society is St Andrews based society with the aim of making feminist issues relevant and accessible for everyone. Read a recent article about the St Andrews Feminist Society in Owl Eyes Magazine or visit their facebook page @standrewsfeminists to find out more.
